CC Project 2 - Skills/Products Exchange

This is an online platform that enables a barter system for consumers who are willing to trade products instead of fiscal money. In simple terms, the users who possess goods and skills can barter it with other consumers. Let us say a user is good at playing soccer and wants to learn driving can trade his soccer skills with another user who is willing to teach driving to him. This website provides the customers with a platform to display, share and acquire the goods and services, unlike the existing online platforms that only provide buying and selling products and services.
